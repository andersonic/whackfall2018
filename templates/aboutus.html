Who Are We?

Songbyrd was created by five Wellesley College students. We have seen the widespread distribution of misinformation about activists online. Songbyrd exists as a tool to combat these willful attacks on activists, and to allow individuals fighting for social justice to control their own online presence. Songbyrd gives activists an opportunity to reclaim our own stories from those who would demonize us online.
